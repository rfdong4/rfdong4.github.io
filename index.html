About Me

Education & Background

I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student at UC Santa Cruz, proudly maintaining a 3.9 GPA and consistently earning a place on the Dean's List. Currently, I serve as a Computer Architecture Researcher at UCSC, where I develop gem5 labs and contribute to education-impact research.

Based in Elk Grove, California, I'm passionate about embedded systems, digital logic, and performance analysis. My academic and personal projects reflect my commitment to understanding both hardware and software aspects of computing systems.

My goal is to leverage my expertise in computer architecture and embedded systems to develop innovative solutions that bridge theoretical concepts with practical applications.
